3x3 Durrës


3x3 Durrës is one of the first 3-on-3 basketball events organised in this city. Approved by FIBA3x3 and FIBA's program FIBA Time-Out 2.0 it provides a "battlefield" for the basketball gladiators from Albania, Kosovo and other countries. Our aim is offering the best that the city has to offer including the sea, the promenade and high-quality basketball; Promoting the newest Olympic Sport to the young generation; Bringing something new to the table for tourist attraction.
The tournament puts twelve teams against each-other in four groups of three teams. The top two teams in the group are qualified for the next stage, The Quarterfinals. After this stage come the Semifinals where four teams clash against each-other to reach the Finals where the winner takes the prize. There are also two other competitions organized: The 3-point challenge and The Slam-Dunk challenge.

3x3 basketball is not like the game of basketball you are used to. It's like a totally different sport. If you want to learn more about the rules and how it's played, click here!

The game ball used in 3x3 is unorthodox too. It's made of material and design that helps the player have a better grip of the ball. For more info on the ball click here!

events

July 2021

The first edition of 3x3 Durrës took place on July 10th in "Taulantia Promenade". 12 teams participated, two of which came from Kosovo and the other teams from Albania.
Before the Semifinals, a 3-point contest was held were Denis Veselagu from "Goga Basket" won the trophy.
The Semifinals put four teams against each-other, two Kosovo teams favorites to win against two Albanian teams from Durres and Shkodra. After a ton of spectacle and hard-fought games, The Final game arrived with the two Albanian teams competing against each-other. The team winner of the first edition was "The Answer" from Shkodra city.
